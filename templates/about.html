About Bluelist

Bluelist was created to make talented local freelancers accessible to everyone.

Freelancers

Freelancers can create a profile that explains the service they offer and provide references or examples of their work through links and uploaded files.

Looking for a Freelancer?

On Bluelist you can easily find freelancers that are available in your city and then decide which ones to contact based on examples of their work.
